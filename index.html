Frequently Asked Questions

What is the best time to visit the snooker club?

The best time to visit is during weekdays, especially before 5 PM, when the club is generally less crowded. For a relaxed experience, early mornings or late evenings are ideal.

Do I need to book a table in advance?

While walk-ins are welcome, we recommend booking a table in advance, especially during weekends or evenings, to ensure availability.

Are there any membership options available?

Yes, we offer various membership options with exclusive perks like priority booking, discounts, and free access to club events. Please contact us for detailed plans.

Do you provide snooker lessons for beginners?

Absolutely! Our experienced coaches offer personalized lessons for beginners and intermediate players. Group and individual sessions are available.

What are the operating hours of the club?

Our club is open from 10 AM to 11 PM every day, including weekends and holidays.

Is there a dress code for the club?

We maintain a smart casual dress code. Please avoid wearing sports shorts, flip-flops, or sleeveless shirts.
